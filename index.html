About

I am a PhD student at the University of Cambridge. My research investigates speeding up CFD simulations and signed distance field generation. I look at GPU utilisation in modelling high-speed flow and shock waves in regular rectilinear grids with embedded cut cell geometries.

This page documents examples from my research and some of my creative coding side projects. These include interactive simulations and interesting graphics algorithms often inspired by tutorials and code examples found online.
