About

I am a PhD student at the University of Cambridge. My research investigates speeding up CFD simulations and signed distance field generation. I look at GPU utilisation in modelling high-speed flow and shock waves in regular rectilinear grids with embedded cut cell geometries.

This page features coding projects from my research or based on concepts which I have found interesting. Most of these are inspired by tutorials and code examples found online. The page includes interactive simulations, graphics and other software applications with the aim of documenting some of my side projects.
